FAQ

Q) How do I return my Levi.com order?

You can return your Levi.com purchases online or to a Levi's Retail Store or Levi's Outlet. If you used PayPal, you can return online for a full refund or in-store for store credit. There are three ways to make your Levi.com return:
- Use the prepaid , pre-addressed USPS or UPS shipping label included with your order to ship your return $7.50 will be deducted from your refund.
- Mail by a carrier of your choice (and at your own expense) to: Levi.com
Attn: Returns Department 100 Precision Drive Walton, KY 41094
- Return to any Levi's Retail Store, along with your original packing slip and/or a copy of your Shipping Confirmation email or online purchase receipt. Items returned without a receipt are eligible for store credit only.

Q) How (and how soon) will I receive my refund?

Your original form of payment will be refunded within 30 days of receiving your return. Gift returns will be refunded to the original purchaser. Items without receipts are not eligible for refunds but may be eligible for store credit.

Q) What if my order is damaged?

If you receive a damaged or defective item, get in touch with our Customer Service team right away and we'll send you a replacement (provided we hear from you before an item has sold out). You can reach the team at +91-8935020870. If you'd rather return the item, call us so that we can make sure you don't have to pay for return shipping.

If an item proves defective in workmanship or materials within 14 days, our Customer Service team will be happy to assist you. This applies only to normal wear and does not apply to misuse, accidental damage, or changing your mind. Before contacting Customer Service, have your order number ready - you can find your order number by logging in to your Levi.com account. And of course, please be ready to describe the damage or defect.

Q) Help! I keep missing my delivery. What should I do?

If you miss your delivery, the carrier will let you know when they'll try to re-deliver. After three delivery attempts, you'll need to contact the carrier about picking up your delivery at a carrier depot. If you aren't able to collect your delivery from the depot, it will be sent back to Levi's eShop Returns department.

Q) How do I know that my Levi's jeans are the real deal?

We want to protect the integrity of our amazing brand, so Levi Strauss & Co. is committed to enforcing its trademark and property rights in order to prevent the sale of counterfeit products bearing its trademark or designs. However, we're aware of counterfeit Levi's products in the marketplace. In order to avoid purchasing a counterfeit item, we recommend that you purchase your jeans from Levi.com or from one of our authorized Levi's dealers.
Characteristics of counterfeit products will vary on a case by case basis, but there are two trademarks of genuine Levi's jeans:
1. The Tab Device trademark on the back right pocket
2. The Arcuate Stitching Design trademark (or "double arcs) on the back pockets
